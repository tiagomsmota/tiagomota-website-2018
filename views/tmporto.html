Teatro Municipal do Porto

Website redesign for the Municipal Theatre of Oporto, based on modern design concepts and guidelines

This project does not consist on the official Teatro Municipal do Porto website and I do not hold any formal relationship with the institution.

This project consists on a website redesign for the Teatro Municipal do Porto.

The main purpose was to create a website that serves a better information architecture and visual order, while keeping the authentic traits and stylistic identifiers of the institution.

The lack of an strong, clean and modern digital identity on the institution's website was the main motivator for this project.

The current Teatro Municipal do Porto website can be found here

Design

The trend on artistic institutions, particularly theatres, was analyzed and taken into consideration for this redesign.

A balance between a bold artistic identity and an effective, clear communication was pursued.

Neutral contrasts were used to provide a sense of bold elegance to the user. Inconsolata and PT Sans Narrow was the elected typography combination.

The interface was designed using Gravit.
