Rocket Jobs

A portuguese job board dedicated to establish the connection between young talents and companies in the IT world.

Rocket Jobs grew from my personal goal of creating a full stack application product for pure practice.

The IT world is one of the most promising fields in the modern world, where talent and innovation drives business development and, therefore, growth.

From this premise Rocket Jobs was born - conceived to establish a connection between fresh talents looking for a first opportunity in the portuguese IT job market and companies willing to hire young talents and embrace innovation.

Design

A sharp, clean and minimalist interface was conceived to achieve the most enjoyable user experience and focus possible, free of unnecessary visual noise.

The use of a strong and youthful primary color was essential to achieve an energetic, positive feel and to mark highlights and focus points.

Montserrat was the elected font for the project, contributing to the modern and minimalist feel of the overall design. Josefin Sans was used for the logo.

Gravit was used for the interface design.

Development

Rocket Jobs development is still in progress.

This project, in terms of development, serves as a playground for experimenting with multiple concepts and technologies.

The development is made under an MVC Framework using Node.js, Express and MongoDB. Stylesheets are handled with SASS and Grunt.

The project is currently deployed on Heroku's free tier, for showcase. The source code is pushed to a GitHub repository, open for the public to consult.
